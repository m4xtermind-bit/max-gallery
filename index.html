Beautiful Moments

Hi, I'm Max.

Columbus, OH (open to relocate)

Fueled by a love for the world-building in film and video games, I’ve always had a curiosity across art forms, moving between illustration, 3D, and music. Each medium taught me something the others couldn’t, and together they’ve given me an unconventional mix of reference points to draw from: the wildly dramatic and fantastical, the technical, and the ordinary moments of my everyday life.

Architecture at OSU taught me to think about how things are experienced, not just how they look, and that lens applies to everything I make.
